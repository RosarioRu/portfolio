About Me

My interest in programming began when I taught myself a little bit of Python in order to tutor my little sister who was failing her high school Computer Science class. I had no idea what coding was, let alone how much I would love it. My work after college centered around healthcare, as a scheduler, receptionist, and electronic health records trainer. Although I loved working with amazing people, I knew I was misisng something and tutoring my little sister made that clear. I was missing building complex things and tackling problems I could spend hours working at. This is what has lead me to pursue a new career in programming by taking classes at Epicodus.
